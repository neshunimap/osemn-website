Introduction

Data science methodologies provide structured approaches for solving data-driven problems. This website presents the OSEMN methodology, a practical framework widely used in real-world data science projects. It helps teams gather data, clean it, discover patterns, build predictive models, and communicate results, making complex processes more understandable and manageable.
